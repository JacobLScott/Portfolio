Chimken Crusade

April 2023

Role: Designer, Programmer, Narrative

Chimken Crusade is an accessible bullet hell game where players are armed with naught but their wits and Chimken the chicken's stubby legs. With the support of an wise old goat, players must escort a golden egg back to Chimken's home without falling prey to the jealous monsters.

This game was developed in Unity for a class over the course of a semester by myself and a partner. It was originally planned to be a 2D Tactical RPG featuring an autoscrolling camera to incentivize players to make their moves quickly, until we realized we needed to adjust the scope. The project was then successfully pivoted into the game you see here.

Contributions

  • Cooperated with a partner to plan, prototype and develop ~20 minute game within the span of 12 weeks

  • Programmed and prototyped the main autoscrolling camera mechanic

  • Programmed the player movement, health bar, dialogue system, and 4 different enemy types

  • Created 4+ levels using Unity's tilemap system

  • Wrote all dialogue and tutorials
