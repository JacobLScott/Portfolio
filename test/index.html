About Me

Hi! I'm Jacob, a game designer and user experience researcher from New Jersey. I've been making and touuching up games since middle school. I have a fascination for turn-based tactics games and am about to wrap up development on one through an opportunity at the USC Games program. I am graduating this semester from USC with a BA in Interactive Media and Game Development.

Here are a few of the projects I've had the opportunity to work on throughout my college career, as well as some personal projects I've developed for fun. I am primarily interested in the moment-to-moment gameplay and decisions that people make while playing games and hope to create engaging games that challenge players' critical-thinking skills.
